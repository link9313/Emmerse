What is the Emmerse?

The Emmerse is a new way of viewing concerts and sports. You can immerse yourself in the environment to accurately portray that you are there. You nolonger have to wish you could see and event as if you were there. You can actually be "there" and enjoy the environment and the event as if you were there.

How Does it Work?

The Emmerse is an app that uses the technology of the Oculus Rift so the user can virtually be where they wish to be. The Oculus Rift provides the virtual environment that makes the user feel as if they are watching the concert in person or are personally at the football game. Instead of only being able to watch the game on TV, people can now freely feel the same experience as if you were at the game.
